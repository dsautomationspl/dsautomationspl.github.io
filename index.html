Common Questions

Do I need to pay a monthly subscription for your services?

No. We typically charge a one-time project fee for building the automation. However, you will need to pay for your own software subscriptions (e.g., Zapier ($20/mo) or Make ($9/mo)). We will help you choose the most cost-effective plan.

What happens if the automation breaks?

We include a 30-day support period with every project to fix any bugs for free. After that, we offer optional maintenance packages, or we can train you on how to fix simple issues yourself.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We build the automations inside *your* accounts. We never hold your data on our servers. Once the project is done, you can revoke our access and the system keeps running.
